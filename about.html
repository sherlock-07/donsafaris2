Our Story

In the heart of Africa, where the wild beats of nature compose a symphony of awe-inspiring beauty, Don Odyssey Safaris was born – a testament to the dream of unveiling the untouched wilderness of Tanzania to the world. Founded by Donald Elisante , Don Odyssey Safaris emerged not just as a travel company but as a bridge to the extraordinary, connecting souls to st pristine landscapes and magnificent wildlife. Our journey began with a simple yet profound realization that the true essence of a safari lies not in merely observing but in deeply connection with the natural world. It’s about creating a dialogue with nature, understanding its rhythms, and respecting its wild heart. This realization shaped our mission to offer not just safaris but transformative experiences that inspire a lasting love and respect for nature.

At Don Odyssey Safaris, we believe that every expedition should be as unique as the individuals embarking on it. Our bespoke safaris are tailor-made to immerse our guests in the breath-taking beauty of Tanzania, from the sweeping plains of the Serengeti to the majestic peaks of Kilimanjaro and the hidden havens of the Ngorongoro Crater. With Don Odyssey, travellers don’t just visit, they become part of the story, the legacy, and the magic of Africa. Sustainability and conservation are the cornerstones of our ethos. We are deeply committed to preserving the natural and cultural heritage of the places we explore. Through responsible tourism, community engagement, and conservation partnerships, we strive to make a positive impact, ensuring that the wonders of Tanzania will be cherished for generations to come.

Our story is one of passion, adventure, and unwavering commitment to make every safari an unforgettable journey of discovery. Don Odyssey Safaris invites you to be a part of this story, to explore, dream, and discover the unparalleled beauty of Africa with us. Join us on this journey, where every moment is an adventure, and every adventure is a step towards preserving the wild beauty of our planet. Welcome to Don Odyssey Safaris – where extraordinary adventures create timeless memories!

Mission

Our mission is to provide exceptional safari experiences that go beyond the ordinary, offering our guests a gateway into the heart of Africa’s natural splendor. We are dedicated to crafting personalized adventures that not only reveal the wonders of Tanzania’s wildlife and landscapes but also foster a deeper understanding and respect for the natural world. Through our commitment to excellence, sustainability, and unparalleled service, we aim to create journeys that inspire, educate, and leave a lasting positive impact on both our guests and the environment.

Vision of Don Odyssey Safaris

Our vision is to become the leading safari operator in Africa, renowned for our ability to connect people with nature in profound and meaningful ways. We strive to set the standard for responsible and sustainable tourism, preserving the continent’s precious ecosystems for future generations while enhancing the economic and social well-being of local communities. We envision a world where travel is a force for good, empowering people to become ambassadors for conservation and cultural appreciation.

Values of Don Odyssey Safaris

We believe in creating genuine and immersive experiences that reflect the true spirit of Africa and committed to responsible tourism practices that prioritize conservation and the well-being of local ecosystems and communities.
We pursue the highest standards in all our services, ensuring every guest enjoys comfort, safety, and the adventure of a lifetime, as we also value the power of learning and discovery, aiming to enlighten our guests about the natural world and the importance of its preservation.
We also operate with a deep respect for the wildlife, cultures, and traditions of the regions we explore, promoting cultural exchange and understanding.
