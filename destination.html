The best parks to visit on a safari in Tanzania.

When planning a safari in Tanzania, think about the differences between the popular northern circuit and the more tranquil southern route. The northern circuit features the renowned Serengeti National Park, where you can also visit the Ngorongoro Crater for an unforgettable wildlife experience. For a less crowded adventure, the southern circuit is ideal, offering hidden gems like Ruaha National Park. Don’t miss the chance to unwind on Zanzibar’s beautiful beaches, the perfect spot for some relaxation in paradise.
